# Creative Challenge: Accelerate Personal Productivity with AI

In this activity, you will follow some simple steps that, in the end, will help create clear and useful prompts using AI.


## Step 1: Define the intention

What I want the AI to do, for whom, and with what expected result.

- **Content type**: A realistic weekly schedule / action plan
- **Audience**: You (a professional who works hybrid, studies online, and wants to exercise more)
- **Goal**: Fit exercise into weekdays without burning out, while working around your husband's evening classes and Friday fatigue


> I want the AI to generate a realistic weekly exercise plan for me (a person who works hybrid, studies online, and wants to exercise more), with the goal of fitting exercise into weekdays without causing excessive fatigue, respecting my routine and my husband's routine.

**Weekly Exercise Plan**
This plan keeps things realistic, low-pressure, and flexible. It builds on your existing morning routine where it works, adds lighter options on office days and Friday, and avoids evenings after 7pm, weekends, and anything that would interfere with your classes, study time, or your husband’s fatigue. Total exercise is 3–4 days per week with rest or very light activity built in. Sessions are 30–60 minutes max.

**Quick Tips for Success**

- *Flexibility*: If a morning feels off, shift to the evening slot (before 7pm) or shorten the session. Study time can move slightly if needed.
- *Intensity*: Keep it moderate — focus on consistency over perfection. Mix strength (2x/week), walking/cardio, and mobility.
- *Fatigue management*: Prioritize sleep and nutrition. Friday is intentionally light/no-pressure.
- *Tracking*: Use a simple note or app to check off what you did — no guilt if you miss one.

This should help you exercise more without burning out or disrupting your hybrid work, classes, or home life. Adjust as real life happens!
