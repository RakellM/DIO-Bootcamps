## Step 3: Combine everything into a final prompt

**FINAL PROMPT**

I want a list of vitamins that are good to be consumed by adults between age 30 to 50 with the objective to sumplemt their diet. Consider the context: weekly routine. The content should be a list per weekday and time of day (such as morning, aftenoon, evening and night), containing the recomended daily dosage, how it should be taken (with water, food) and why in a single sentence. Avoid over explanation and advices.


**Best Format**

Create a weekly vitamin supplementation plan for healthy adults ages 30–50. Organize the output as a list by weekday (Monday through Sunday) and by time of day (morning, afternoon, evening, night). For each entry, include:
- Vitamin name
- Recommended daily dosage
- How to take it (with water, with food, empty stomach)
- A single-sentence explanation of why it helps in the context of diet supplementation

Keep it factual, concise, and avoid over-explanation or general wellness advice.
